Our Story

Serving Quality products
since 1963

Rooted since 1963 on farmer-centred values, Kiambaa Dairy Farmers Co-operative produces high-quality dairy items that are ethically, hygienically, and sustainably made from farm to shelf. With farmers' improved lives at heart, we transform fresh milk into trusted brands of Dafina yogurt, mala, and long-life whole milk. Taste the trust. Try Dafina today.
